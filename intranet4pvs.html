Responsibilities
HR OFFICER is there to help with organizing your life both within and outside the department, so that you can fully focus on your research and teaching duties. Among others, you can search the full processing or professional advice on: prolong your visa, welcome visitors, hire (unhire) a team member, sign up for an education activity, getting meal tickets
STUDY OFFICER Covers services related to dealing with students on all levels, including PhD’s. He/she is there to help you with approaching students and organize the things around teaching and learning. Go there, when you need enroll PhD student, approach students, help with your teaching duties, help with your help with your study duties. Dedicated page is here
PR OFFICER When you want to bring attention of the students, research colleagues or general public to what you do, approach him or her to search for the right solution of form, visual and/or organization of an event. Go there when you need to present your or your team’s results to general public, present your or your team’s results to fellow colleagues, organize a workshop, organize a conference, hold a meeting at CTU etc.
OPERATIONS They are here to deal all other matters excluded HR and STUDY. Whether you need to discuss finances, equipment, traveling, intellectual property rights or painting of your office, go there. You may also need to: send a package or official letter, go to to conference / business trip, buy equipment, services or books, publish a paper, print materials, find a research lab, care for IT infrastructure
FINANCIAL DEPT. - Anything related to money past, current or future - go there. In GRANTS you may need to apply for grant, help with grant you already have. Regardning IPR you may want to set up start-up, find a licencee, licence results of your R&D, help with contractual research you just won, apply for patent?help with IPR issues in general. In BUSINESS DEVELOPMENT you may want to establish partnership, indentify customer.

Stipends
Stipends can be used to reward students for their outstanding study performance. Foreign students can also ask for various kinds of support to cover their living expenses. All the possibilities are currently being mapped, until then you can check the webpage of Faculty and University. All PI's can ask for access to check the stipend income of his/her students here. Contact Jarda Šíp with issues related to stipends.
